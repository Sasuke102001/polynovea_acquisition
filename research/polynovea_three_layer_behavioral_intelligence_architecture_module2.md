# Polynovea — Three-Layer Behavioral Intelligence Architecture

# Purpose

This document defines the three major intelligence layers currently emerging inside the Polynovea ecosystem:

1. Existing Venue Behavioral Intelligence Database
2. Human Preference & Survey Intelligence Layer
3. Future Marketing Interaction & Acquisition Telemetry Layer

Together, these layers form the foundation of a long-term behavioral intelligence and optimization infrastructure.

---

# SYSTEM OVERVIEW

```text
LAYER 1
Venue Behavioral Intelligence
(reviews + operational behavior)

        ↓

LAYER 2
Human Preference Intelligence
(surveys + selection psychology)

        ↓

LAYER 3
Marketing Interaction Intelligence
(content + ads + acquisition telemetry)
```

These layers compound into:

```text
human psychology
↔
venue behavior
↔
acquisition behavior
↔
optimization systems
```
